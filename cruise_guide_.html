Ports of Call & Activities

Labadee, Haiti - Royal Caribbean's Private Destination

What makes it special: Completely safe, controlled environment designed specifically for cruise passengers

Perfect for your family:

  • Dragon's Tail Coaster: Alpine-style mountain coaster perfect for 10-12 year olds
  • Arawak Aqua Park: Floating water park with slides and obstacles (ages 8+)
  • Multiple pristine beaches with calm, clear water
  • All beach amenities included with your cruise (chairs, umbrellas)
  • Cultural shows and local artisan demonstrations

San Juan, Puerto Rico - #1 Ranked Family Port

Why it's the best: Perfect blend of history, culture, and adventure for tweens

Must-do experiences:

  • El Morro & San Cristóbal Forts: 500-year-old Spanish fortresses with tunnels, dungeons, and panoramic views
  • Kite flying tradition: Bring $3-4 for kites to fly on El Morro's grassy field
  • Old San Juan exploration: Colorful colonial buildings, cobblestone streets, free trolley tours
  • El Yunque Rainforest: Only tropical rainforest in US National Forest System
Family advantages: US territory (no passport needed, English spoken, US dollar), most activities free or very affordable, rich history that engages 10-12 year olds

Charlotte Amalie, St. Thomas - World-Class Beaches & Views

Famous for: Magens Bay (one of world's most beautiful beaches) and duty-free shopping

Family highlights:

  • Magens Bay Beach: Calm, shallow waters perfect for kids; small entry fee but worth it
  • Paradise Point Skyride: Cable car to 700-foot summit with amazing views
  • Coral World Ocean Park: Interactive marine experiences, shark encounters, sea turtle feedings
  • Duty-free shopping: Main Street Charlotte Amalie for jewelry, electronics, liquor

Weather & Timing
